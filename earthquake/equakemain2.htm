 On-line Analysis of Earthquake Studies

Please read the disclaimer before using or downloading these materials. By clicking on a study title below, you will open up a new window in which you will be able to choose among several on-line analysis options. It is recommended that you open a codebook window in order to review specific items in the data. The studies were processed for use with SDA. SDA is a set of programs for the documentation and Web-based analysis of survey data.  It also includes procedures for creating customized subsets of data sets.  SDA is developed and maintained by the Computer-assisted Survey Methods Program (CSM) at the University of California, Berkeley.
